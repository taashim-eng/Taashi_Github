# Onboarding Guide (Data Engineering)

## Week 1: Foundations

- Meet the team, manager, and key partners.
- Get access to core tools (GitHub, CI/CD, data warehouse, BI tools).
- Review platform overview and architecture diagrams.
- Complete a guided walkthrough of one end-to-end pipeline.

## Week 2: Environment & Standards

- Set up local development environment.
- Review platform standards:
  - Coding
  - Data modeling
  - Testing
  - Orchestration
- Pair with a senior engineer on a small bug or enhancement.
